The 32 Signs

In 2016, paleoanthropologist Genevieve von Petzinger documented 32 recurring geometric signs across 146 cave sites spanning 30,000 years. Lines, chevrons, circles, spirals — the same symbols from France to Indonesia. Not decoration. A shared cognitive vocabulary predating writing by 25,000 years.

caveLLMan extends this to 88 symbols — adding what a cave painter of the 21st century needs: internet, AI, money, stress, freedom.

BE — The Super-Verb

One circle. Turns any noun into a verb:

BE + fear = to be afraid
BE + love = to love
BE + fire = to burn
BE + free = to be free

How to Read

me BE fear dark not see path
→ I am afraid of the dark, I cannot see the way
woman give child food and love
→ a woman gives a child food and love
